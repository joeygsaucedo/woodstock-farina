## 📐 Image Tips

**Best Sizes:**
- Hero images: 1920x1080px (landscape)
- Pizza photos: 800x800px (square)  
- Event photos: 1200x800px (any orientation works)
- Team photos: 800x600px

**Before uploading**, compress large images at:
- https://tinypng.com/
- https://squoosh.app/

This keeps the site loading fast!
