# Image Guide for Woodstock Farina Website

## Where to Download Images

1. **Original Squarespace Site**: Save images directly from your current site
2. **Instagram**: https://www.instagram.com/woodstock_farina/
   - Right-click images → "Save image as..."
   - Or use a tool like: https://igram.io/ to download Instagram photos

## Image Requirements

**For best quality**:
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Size**: At least 1920x1080px for hero images, 800x600px minimum for others
- **Orientation**: 
  - Hero images: Landscape (horizontal)
  - Pizza photos: Square or portrait works best
  - Event photos: Any orientation

**Optimization Tip**: Before adding, you can compress images at:
- https://tinypng.com/
- https://squoosh.app/

## Quick Download Instructions

### From Instagram:
1. Go to https://www.instagram.com/woodstock_farina/
2. Click on any post
3. Use https://igram.io/ or similar:
   - Copy the Instagram post URL
   - Paste into igram.io
   - Download the image
4. Rename according to this guide

### From Squarespace:
1. Right-click on any image
2. "Save image as..."
3. Rename according to this guide
